About

JB A1 Services Ltd

SGI approved Company which provides truck driver training including class 1A, 3A, map reading, defensive driving, log book, border crossing and air brake

JB A1 Driving School is located very close to downtown Regina. offer new drivers affordable and quality driver education. We are committed to encourage our clients for advanced road training and making Saskatchewan’s roads safe for all users.

  • Job Offer

    • JB A1 driving School have very strong relationship with local companies. Before and and after getting class 1A or 3A license, our successful students get job offer immediately in well reputed companies.

  • Payment Plan

    • JB A1 Driving School offer very comfortable, affordable payment plan for eligible students.

  • Flexible Training Schedule

    • JB A1 Driving School offer weekends and evening classes for odd job workers.

  • Females

    • JB A1 Driving School offers special discount of 10% for females.

  • Affordable Accomodation

    • JB A1 Driving School offers affordable residence with pickup and drop facility to out stationed students.
